What is Thempe?

Thempe (/tempe/) is an acronym of Thematic Mapping Practice and Explanation. Inspired by Tempeh, a Javanese food which is written in Javanese as: ꦠꦺꦩ꧀ꦥꦺ (Tempeh in Wikipedia).
By learning map types in Thempe you will gain more knowledge in the simplest and exciting way. Are you ready for more?
